FAQ


Everything You Need to Know

Are these original Bicycle® Playing Cards?

Yes! Manufactured and printed by USPCC - The United States Playing Card Company - with the patented Air-Cushion Finish. Imported form the U.S.A.

Are these new or used?

New! All of the available products are brand-new, unused, unopened and sealed with the manufacturer's original seal.

Do i have to specify the offers when i order?

No. All orders will be charged according to the offer they can be grouped in whether specified or not. Example: ordering 3 red and 3 blue decks will be charged as 3 Two Packs not as 6 single decks.

Can i order a Two Pack of the same color with the offer price?

Unfortunately no for the time being due to the limited quantity. A Two Pack always comes in two colors (red+blue). Ordering multiples of the same color will be charged according to the price of a single deck each. This is to keep the quantities in stock between the two colors balanced. However, you can order as many Two Packs as you want and all orders with mixed colors will be charged with the lower offer price; as many Two Packs as possible even if not specified by the buyer.
